Cookie Policy

Last updated: February 2026

1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work efficiently and to provide information to the site owners.

2. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for the following purposes:

  • Strictly Necessary Cookies: These are essential for the website to function, including remembering your cookie consent preference.
  • Analytics Cookies: These help us understand how visitors interact with our website by collecting anonymous usage data.

3. Managing Cookies

When you first visit our website, a cookie banner will ask for your consent. You can accept or decline non-essential cookies at any time. You can also control cookies through your browser settings. Please note that disabling certain cookies may affect your experience on our website.

4. Third-Party Cookies

We may use third-party analytics services that set their own cookies. These services help us analyse website traffic. Any data collected is aggregated and anonymous.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

6. Contact

If you have any questions about our use of cookies, please contact us at hello@carpenterssquare.co.uk.
