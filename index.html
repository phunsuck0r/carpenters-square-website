About Us

Technology consultancy with commercial teeth.

Carpenters Square is a technology-focused consultancy based in London with deep experience in consumer lending, fintech, and data-driven decision making. We've optimised marketing spend through cohort analysis, delivered technical due diligence for investors, built bespoke software platforms, and created financial models that boards actually use.

We're not just advisors — we build the software, run the numbers, and ship the product. As active angel investors, we also back early-stage startups with capital and hands-on expertise, helping founders move faster from product-market fit to scale. Our clients range from pre-seed startups to established lending businesses optimising multi-million pound portfolios.
